Install//Emmet//
	1. In SublimeText, run Package Control (CMD+SHIFT+P).
	2. Type "install Package Control" + Enter.
	3. Type "Emmet" and select "Emmet for Sublime Text."
	4. Read notes & restart code editor.


Tags//Shortcuts//
	a. <div> & ALT+.		will generate	<div></div>

	b. <body><div><span> & ALT+. 3x generates	<body><div><span> </span></div></body>
	
	c. section#cover & TAB	will generate	<section id="cover"></section>

	d. div.container & TAB	will generate	<div class="container"></div>

	d1)  .form-group & TAB  generates	<div class="form-group"></div>

	e. link & TAB		will generate	<link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="|">

	f. <thanks to Emmet...>
	section#cover>#cover-caption>.container>.col-sm-10.col-sm-offset-1 & TAB

	will generate:
	
	<section id="cover">
		<div id="cover-caption">
			<div class="container">
				<div class="col-sm-10 col-sm-offset-1"></div>
			</div>
		</div>
	</section>

	g. lorem & TAB		will generate two (2) sentences of Lorem Ipsum text.

	h. lorem100 & TAB	will generate 100 words of Lorem Ipsum text.
